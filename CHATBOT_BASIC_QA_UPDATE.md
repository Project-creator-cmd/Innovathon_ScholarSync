## New Patterns Added (20 Basic Q&A)

### 1. What is a Scholarship?
**Keywords**: what is scholarship, scholarship meaning, define scholarship
**Response**: Explains scholarship as free financial aid that doesn't need to be repaid, awarded based on merit, need, or categories.

### 2. Types of Scholarships
**Keywords**: types of scholarships, different types, scholarship categories
**Response**: Covers merit-based, need-based, category-based, talent-based, subject-specific, and provider-based scholarships.

### 3. How to Find Scholarships
**Keywords**: how to find scholarships, where to find, scholarship search
**Response**: Lists government portals (NSP), platforms (ScholarSync, Buddy4Study), educational institutions, and online search tips.

### 4. Required Documents
**Keywords**: documents for scholarship, required documents, document list
**Response**: Comprehensive list of essential, academic, and additional documents needed for applications.

### 5. Low Income Scholarships
**Keywords**: low income scholarship, need based, financial need
**Response**: Lists government, state, and private scholarships for low-income students with personalized income matching.

### 6. Multiple Scholarships
**Keywords**: multiple scholarships, apply to many, several scholarships
**Response**: Confirms students can apply to multiple scholarships with strategy tips and ScholarSync benefits.

### 7. Scholarship Deadlines
**Keywords**: scholarship deadline, last date, when to apply
**Response**: Provides 2026 timeline for government, state, private, and international scholarships.

### 8. Scholarship Fees/Scams
**Keywords**: scholarship fees, pay to apply, scholarship scam
**Response**: Warns that legitimate scholarships are FREE and provides scam detection tips.

### 9. Engineering Scholarships
**Keywords**: engineering scholarship, btech scholarship, technical scholarship
**Response**: Lists government, PSU/corporate, and private scholarships for engineering students.

### 10. Scholarship Amounts
**Keywords**: scholarship amount, how much money, scholarship value
**Response**: Breaks down scholarship amounts from small (₹2,000-10,000) to full-ride (₹10,00,000+).

### 11. What is NSP?
**Keywords**: what is nsp, nsp portal, national scholarship portal
**Response**: Explains NSP as India's official government scholarship platform and compares with ScholarSync.

### 12. Gap Year Eligibility
**Keywords**: gap year scholarship, gap year eligible, year gap
**Response**: Explains which scholarships accept gap years and how to handle them in applications.

### 13. Scholarship Essay/SOP
**Keywords**: scholarship essay, how to write sop, essay tips
**Response**: Provides structure, writing tips, and dos/don'ts for scholarship essays.

### 14. Study Abroad Scholarships
**Keywords**: study abroad scholarship, international scholarship, overseas scholarship
**Response**: Lists popular scholarships for USA, UK, Europe, Australia, Canada with requirements.

### 15. Income Limits
**Keywords**: income limit, income criteria, family income limit
**Response**: Details income limits for government, private, and state scholarships with personalized matching.

### 16. Girls Scholarships
**Keywords**: girls scholarship, scholarship for girls, women scholarship
**Response**: Lists girls-only and girls-preference scholarships from government, state, and private sources.

### 17. Scholarship Rejection
**Keywords**: scholarship rejected, application rejected, got rejected
**Response**: Provides steps to understand rejection, improve, appeal, and apply to more scholarships.

### 18. Scholarship Taxation
**Keywords**: scholarship tax, taxable, income tax on scholarship
**Response**: Explains that scholarships are tax-free under Section 10(16) with conditions.

### 19. MBBS/Medical Scholarships
**Keywords**: mbbs scholarship, medical scholarship, medicine scholarship
**Response**: Lists government, private, hospital-sponsored, and international medical scholarships.

### 20. When to Start Applying
**Keywords**: when to start applying, how early, scholarship timeline
**Response**: Provides ideal timeline for UG, PG, and current students with month-by-month guide.
